# AgriBloom Farmer's User Manual

Welcome to AgriBloom, the offline-first farm management application designed specifically for Kenyan farmers! This manual will guide you through the core features of the mobile app.

## 1. Getting Started
1. **Open the App:** Tap the AgriBloom icon on your Android device.
2. **Login/Register:** Enter your Mobile Phone Number (e.g., 0712345678).
3. **OTP Verification:** You will receive an SMS with a 4-digit code. Enter it to verify your account. If you don't have an internet connection, SMS fallback verification is supported.

## 2. Using the App Offline
AgriBloom is built to be "Offline-First". 
- You **do not** need an active internet or data connection to use the app in the field.
- You can log expenses, track crops, and view your farm details completely offline.
- When you return to an area with an internet connection, simply open the app. AgriBloom will **automatically synchronize** all your offline data to the cloud in the background.

## 3. Managing Your Farm
1. **Dashboard:** When you log in, the Dashboard gives you a quick summary of your Farm's financial health:
   - Total Expenses (KES)
   - Total Sales (KES)
   - Upcoming Tasks (e.g., "Spray Maize Plot in 3 days")
2. **Adding a Plot:** Go to the "Farms" tab and click "Add Plot". You can enter the size (in Hectares) and the crop type (e.g., Maize, Tomatoes).

## 4. Logging Expenses & Sales
1. Tap the **Log Expense** button.
2. You can enter data in 3 ways:
   - **Manually:** Type the amount, category (e.g., Fertilizer), and description.
   - **Voice Note 🎙️:** Tap and hold the "Hold to Speak" button. Speak naturally (e.g., "I bought two bags of fertilizer for 4500 shillings"). The app's AI will automatically fill in the form!
   - **Receipt Scan 📸:** Tap "Upload Receipt" and take a photo of your Agrovet receipt. The app will automatically extract the total amount.
3. Tap **Save**. The data is saved locally and synced when online.

## 5. M-Pesa Integration
- When logging an expense, you can opt to pay via M-Pesa.
- AgriBloom will trigger an **STK Push** to your phone. Simply enter your M-Pesa PIN on your screen when prompted to authorize the payment.

## 6. Settings & Language
- Tap the "Settings" icon in the top right.
- You can toggle the app language between **English** and **Swahili** at any time.
